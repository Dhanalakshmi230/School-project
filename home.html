LAKSHMI HIGH SCHOOL

 Lakshmi High School Home Page

Lakshmi Union High School District No. 205 is a school district headquartered in Lakshmi, Arizona, United States. The union high school district operates nine comprehensive high schools and serves most of Lakshmi and a portion of Phoenix. It is the second largest high school-only district preceded by Phoenix Union.
